About Us

Established in 1959 in Old Kampala, the Aga Khan High School, Kampala was the first multicultural school in Uganda. The present day school continues to play a unique educational role in the country.


Mission

At Aga Khan, we are dedicated to nurturing the minds, hearts, and spirits of our students, cultivating a vibrant community of lifelong learners and compassionate leaders. Rooted in Uganda's rich cultural heritage and global in our outlook, we strive for academic excellence, embracing diversity, and fostering a collaborative environment where each student is empowered to reach their fullest potential.
